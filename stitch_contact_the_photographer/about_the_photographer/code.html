About Me

Visual
Storyteller

My journey began in the chaotic, vibrant streets of Caracas, seeking order in chaos through the viewfinder. For me, photography isn't merely about freezing a moment in time; it's about interpreting the profound silence that exists between the noise of everyday life. It is an act of preservation and a rebellion against forgetting.

Now based in Lisbon, I bridge the Atlantic gap through visual narratives, exploring themes of migration, memory, and the enduring spirit of urban landscapes. I strive to find the intersection where documentary truth meets artistic expression.

I believe that every photograph is a collaboration between the subject, the light, and the observer's own memories. My work invites you to look closer, to see the beauty in the mundane.
